Top 8 IITs

💰 Total Fees: ₹2 – ₹3 Lakhs (Highly Subsidized)

AIIMS Delhi

All India Institute of Medical Sciences

💰 Total Fees: ₹10,000 – ₹30,000 total
🎯 Cutoff (NEET): MBBS: Top 50 Rank in India
👉 Most competitive exam in India

JNU Delhi

🎯 Cutoff: CUET PG: Top Percentile
👉 Competitive for Arts & Humanities

Delhi University

💰 Annual Fees: ₹5,000 – ₹25,000
🎯 Cutoff (CUET): Top Colleges: 98–100 Percentile

BHU / Jadavpur

BITS Pilani

Top Private Tech Institution
